## 🎨 Engineering Philosophy

#### 🛫 Safety-Critical Mindset
> In defense and avionics, software is not just an application; it is a survival tool. I am focused on transitioning my skills towards writing clean, deterministic logic that operates flawlessly under strict physical and timing constraints.

#### ⚙️ The Digital-Physical Bridge
> Fascinated by the exact moment code escapes the screen and interacts with the real world. From moving a physical flap on a wing to reading raw telemetry data off a sensor array, I want to build technology that fundamentally interacts with physics.

#### 🏎️ High-Performance Mechanics
> Inspired by the precision of Formula 1 Engineering. Mastering optimization under environmental constraints, analyzing split-second hardware decision making, and learning how to safely push embedded systems to their absolute limits.
